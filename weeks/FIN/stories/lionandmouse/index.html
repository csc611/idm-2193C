There was once a brown Mouse.

Mouse was going home when he ran by Lion, who was sleeping.

Lion woke up and pounced on Mouse.

"Please let me live," said Mouse.

"I am only a small, brown mouse! If you let me live, maybe I will be able to help you in the future one day."

The Lion laughed at Mouse. How could a small, brown mouse help her, the Lion?

A few days later, Lion got caught in a hunter's trap. She was tied to a tree, sad and hurt.

Just then, Mouse passed by.

He saw her in the trap and start to chew away the ropes.

Lion was free!

"We should help each other, whoever it be, no matter the size."
