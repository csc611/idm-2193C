One hot summer day, a bald man (named Bald) sat on a log.

A fly buzzed by and saw Bald.

The fly kept teasing Bald and his head.

Bald tried to swat Fly, but missed, and smacked his own head!

"Ouch!" yelped Bald.

Fly laughed and buzzed more.

Bald kept swinging his arms in the air, but kept hitting his own head!

"You only get hurt when you give your enemies more attention."
