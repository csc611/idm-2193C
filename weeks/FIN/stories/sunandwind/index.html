Once, the Wind and the Sun were arguing about who was stronger.

"Surely I am the stronger one," said the Wind.

Then, they saw a traveller come down the path.

"I know how to find out who is stronger, once and for all," said the Sun.

"Whoever can make the traveller take off his cloak is the stronger one. You can start first, Wind."

The Sun moved behind the clouds, and the Wind began to huff and puff at the traveller.

The harsher the Wind blew, the more the traveller wrapped his cloak around him, shivering.

After a while, the Wind said sadly, "I must give up now."

Just then, the Sun came out from beind the clouds and shone her warm light on the traveller.

The traveller smiled in joy at the shining sun and took off his cloak.

"Kindness has more effects than harshness."
